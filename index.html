Welcome to Central Hotel, Maskeliya

Nestled in the picturesque town of Maskeliya, Sri Lanka, Central Hotel
has been a beacon of hospitality and culinary excellence for over 40 years.
Established with a passion for providing exceptional service and authentic
cuisine, Central Hotel has become a cherished destination for both locals
and travelers alike. Our rich history and commitment to quality have earned
us a reputation as a premier establishment in the heart of Maskeliya.

At Central Hotel, we offer a diverse menu featuring traditional South Indian
and Sri Lankan dishes, meticulously prepared to delight your taste buds.

Join us at Central Hotel and experience the warm hospitality and rich flavors
that have made us a beloved institution for four decades. We look forward to
welcoming you to our home in Maskeliya.
